Grigory Solomatov

Researcher in Underwater Computer Vision

About

 I earned a PhD in 2021 from the Technical University of Denmark, where I worked on asymptotically fast algorithms for algebraic error-correcting codes under the guidance of Johan Rosenkilde and Peter Beelen. Currently, I'm a postdoc in the COLOR Lab at the university of Haifa, where I work with Assistant Professor Derya Akkaynak. In my research, I investigate how useful information can be extracted from underwater images, which includes topics like restoring colors in underwater scenes as well as estimating optical properties of the water itself.
